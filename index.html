Who We Are

About the Fraternity

Tau Gamma Phi – Triskelion Grand Fraternity is one of the most respected and influential fraternities in the Philippines. Our Asia 2 Community Chapter carries forward the same founding values of brotherhood, loyalty, and genuine service to our community.

Our fraternity is built on three pillars represented by the Triskelion symbol: Academic Excellence, Community Service, and Brotherhood. These values guide every Triskelion in their personal and professional lives.

Locally, our Asia 2 chapter is dedicated to uplifting the community through meaningful programs, including our monthly Community Clean-Up Drive and Free Breakfast Program — tangible proof that true brotherhood extends beyond our own ranks.

Our Legacy

Chapter History

Founding

Asia 2 Community Chapter Established

Our chapter was founded by a dedicated group of brothers who believed in bringing the Triskelion spirit to the heart of our community. The founding members pledged to serve with honor and integrity.

Early Years

Building the Brotherhood

The chapter steadily grew as more brothers joined the cause. Strong bonds were forged through shared experiences, community activities, and a common commitment to the fraternity's core values.

Milestone

First Community Outreach

The chapter launched its first formal community outreach efforts, establishing the foundation for the service programs that continue to this day. Brothers united to make a tangible difference in the lives of our neighbors.

Monthly

Clean-Up Drive Launched

The Asia 2 chapter formalized its monthly Community Clean-Up Drive, rallying brothers and residents alike to maintain the cleanliness and beauty of our streets, parks, and waterways.

Monthly

Free Breakfast Program

Driven by compassion, the chapter launched its monthly Free Breakfast Program, providing hot and nutritious meals to underprivileged families and individuals. Brothers personally prepare and serve every meal.

Present

Growing Stronger

With 52 active brothers and counting, the Asia 2 Community Chapter continues to deepen its roots in service, uphold the dignity of the brotherhood, and inspire future Triskelions.

Our Projects

Monthly
Environment

Community Clean-Up Drive

Every month, our brothers gather to clean our community's streets, esteros, and public spaces. We believe a clean community reflects a disciplined brotherhood. Participants also engage with residents to promote proper waste segregation and environmental responsibility.

Every Month, Without Fail
Monthly
Social Welfare

Libreng Almusal

Walang magugutom sa aming pamayanan. Every month, our brothers personally prepare and serve hot, nutritious meals to underprivileged families and individuals in our community. It is more than food — it is a message that they are not alone, and that the brotherhood cares.
